id,text [000100 (1)]," Her Eadgar wæs, Engla waldend, corðre to cyninge gehalgod on ðære ealdan byrig, Acemannesceastre; eac hi igbuend oðre worde beornas Baðan nemnaþ." [000200 (5)]," Þær wæs blis micel on þam eadgan dæge eallum geworden, <þone> niða bearn nemnað and cigað Pentecostenes dæg." [000300 (8)]," Þær wæs preosta heap, micel muneca ðreat, mine gefrege, gleawra gegaderod." [000400 (10)]," And ða agangen wæs tyn hund wintra geteled rimes fram gebyrdtide bremes cyninges, leohta hyrdes, buton ðær to lafe þa wæs wintergeteles, þæs ðe gewritu secgað, seofon and twentig; swa neah wæs sigora frean ðusend aurnen, ða þa ðis gelamp." [000500 (17)]," And him Eadmundes eafora hæfde nigon and XX, niðweorca heard, wintra on worulde, <ða> þis geworden wæs, and þa on ðam XXX wæs ðeoden gehalgod."
