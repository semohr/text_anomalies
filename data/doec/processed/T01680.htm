[000500 (8)]," Æla, frea beorhta, folkes scippend." [000600 (9)]," Gemilsa þyn mod me to gode, sile þyne are <þynum> earminge." [000700 (11)]," Se byð earming þe on eorðan her dæiges and nihtes deofle campað and hys willan wyrcð; wa him þære mirigðe, þonne he ða handlean hafað and sceawað, bute he þæs yfeles ær geswyce." [000800 (16)]," Se byð eadig, se þe on eorðan her dæiges and nyhtes drihtne hyræð and a hys willan wyrcð: wel hym þæs geweorkes, ðonne he ða handlean hafað and sceawað, gyf he ealteawne ende gedreogeð." [000900 (21)]," Æla, leohtes leoht." [001000 (21)]," Æla, lyfes wynn." [001100 (22)]," Getiþa me, tireadig kyning, þonne ic minre sawle swegles bydde, ece are." [001200 (24)]," Þu eart eaðe god, hæfst and waldest ana ofer ealle eorðan and heofonas syddra gesceafta." [001300 (27)]," Ðu eart soð meotod, ana ofer ealle eorðbugende, swilce on heofonum up þu eart hælend god." [001400 (30)]," Ne mæg þe aherian hæleða ænig; þeh us gesomnie geond sidne grund, men ofer moldan, geond ealne middaneard, ne mage we næfre asæcgan, ne þæt soðe witan, hu þu æðele eart, ece drihten." [001500 (35)]," Ne þeah engla werod up on heofenum snotra tosomne sæcgan ongunnon, ne magon hy næfre areccean, ne þæt gerim wytan, hu þu mære eart, mihtig drihten." [001600 (39)]," Ac is wunder mycel, wealdend engla, gif þu hit sylfa wast, sigores ealdor, hu þu mære eart, mihtig and mægenstrang, ealra kyninga kyning, Crist , ealra worulda scippend, wealdend engla, ealra dugeþa duguð, drihten hælend." [001700 (45)]," Ðu eart se æðela þe on ærdagum ealra femnena wyn fægere akende on Bethleem ðære byrig beornum to frofre, eallum to are ylda bearnum, þam þe gelyfað on lyfiendne god and on þæt ece leoht uppe on roderum." [001800 (51)]," Ðyn mægen ys swa mære, mihtig drihten, swa þæt ænig ne wat eorðbuende þa deopnesse drihtnes mihta, ne þæt ænig ne wat engla hades þa heahnisse heofena kyninges." [001900 (56)]," Ic þe andette, ælmihtig god, þæt ic gelyfe on þe, leofa hælend, þæt þu eart se miccla and se mægenstranga and se eadmoda ealra goda and se ece kyning ealra gesceafta, and ic eom se litla for þe and se lyðra man, se her syngige swiðe genehhe, dæges and nihtes do, swa ic ne sceolde, hwile mid weorce, hwile mid worde, hwile mid geþohte, þearle scyldi, inwitniðas oft and gelome." [002000 (67)]," Ac ic þe halsige nu, heofena drihten, and gebidde me to þe, bearna selost, þæt ðu gemilsige me, mihtig drihten, heofena heahkyning and se halga gast, and gefylste me, fæder ælmihtig, þæt ic þinne willan gewyrcean mæge, ær ic of þysum lyfe gehweorfe." [002100 (74)]," Ne forweorn þu me, wuldres drihten, ac getyþa me, tyreadig kyning, læt me mid englum up siðian, sittan on swegle, herian heofonas god haligum reorde a butan ende." [002200 (79)], Amen.
