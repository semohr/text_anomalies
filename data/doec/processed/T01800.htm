id,text [000100 (1)], Ðis is nu se wæterscipe ðe us wereda god to frofre gehet foldbuendum. [000200 (3)]," He cwæð ðæt he wolde ðæt on worulde forð of ðæm innoðum a libbendu wætru fleowen, ðe wel on hine gelifden under lyfte." [000300 (6)]," Is hit lytel tweo ðæt ðæs wæterscipes welsprynge is on hefonrice, ðæt is halig gæst." [000400 (9)]," Ðonan hine hlodan halge and gecorene, siððan hine gierdon ða ðe gode herdon ðurh halga bec hider on eorðan geond manna mod missenlice." [000500 (13)]," Sume hine weriað on gewitlocan, wisdomes stream, welerum gehæftað, ðæt he on unnyt ut ne tofloweð." [000600 (16)], Ac se wæl wunað on weres breostum ðurh dryhtnes giefe diop and stille. [000700 (18)]," Sume hine lætað ofer landscare riðum torinnan; nis ðæt rædlic ðing, gif swa hlutor wæter, hlud and undiop, tofloweð æfter feldum oð hit to fenne werð." [000800 (22)]," Ac hladað iow nu drincan, nu iow dryhten geaf ðæt iow Gregorius gegiered hafað to durum iowrum dryhtnes welle." [000900 (25)]," Fylle nu his fætels, se ðe fæstne hider kylle brohte, cume eft hræðe." [001000 (27)]," Gif her ðegna hwelc ðyrelne kylle brohte to ðys burnan, bete hine georne, ðy læs he forsceade scirost wætra, oððe him lifes drync forloren weorðe."
