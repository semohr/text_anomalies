[012500 (214.236)], Þa ða se hæþengylda þæt geseah: þa astrehte he hine to Iohannes fotum: & syþðan ferde to þam heahgerefan & him þa wundra mid hludre stemne cydde. [012600 (214.239)], Hi ða begen þone apostol gesohton: his miltsunge biddende. [012700 (214.240)], Ða bead se apostol him syfan nihta fæsten. & hi syððan gefullode. & hi æfter ðam fulluhte towurpon eal hyra deofulgyld & mid heora maga fultume & mid eallum cræfte arærdon gode mære cyrcan on þæs apostoles wurðmynte. [012800 (214.244)], Þa ða se apostol wæs nigon & hundnigontig geara þa æteowede him drihten crist mid þam oðrum apostolum þe he of þysum life genumen hæfde: & cwæð. [012900 (214.246)], Iohannes: cum to me tima is þæt ðu mid þinum gebroðrum wistfullige on minum gebeorscipe. [013000 (214.247)], Iohannes þa aras: & eode wið þæs hælendes: ac he him tocwæð. [013100 (214.248)], Nu on sunnandæg mines æristes dæge þu cymst to me: & æfter þam worde drihten gewende to heofenum. [013200 (215.250)], Se apostol miclum blissode on þam behate: & on þam sunnanuhtan ærwacel to ðære cyrcan com. & þam folce fram hancrede oð undern godes riht lærde. & him mæssan gesang. & cwæð ðæt se hælend hine on þam dæge to heofenum gelaðod hæfde. [013300 (215.253)], Het ða delfan his byrgene wið ðam weofode. & þæt greot utawegan. & he eode cucu & gesund into his byrgene: & astrehtum handum to gode clypode. [013400 (215.256)], Drihten crist. ic þancige ðe þæt ðu me gelaðodest to þinum wistum: þu wast þæt ic mid ealre heortan þe gewilnode. [013500 (215.257)], Oft ic þe bæd þæt ic moste to þe faran: ac ðu cwæde þæt ic andbidode þæt ic ðe mare folces gestrynde. [013600 (215.259)], Ðu heolde minne lichaman wið ælcere besmitennysse: & þu symle mine sawle onlihtest. & me nahwar ne forlete. [013700 (215.260)], Ðu settest on minum muðe þinre soðfæstnysse word. & ic awrat þa lare ðe ic of þinum muþe gehyrde. & þa wundra ðe ic þe wyrcan geseah. [013800 (215.263)], Nu ic ðe betæce drihten þine bearn þa ðe þin gelaðung mæden & moder. þurh wæter. & þone halgan gast þe gestrynde. [013900 (215.264)], Onfoh me to minum gebroðrum: mid þam ðe ðu come & me gelaðodest. [014000 (215.265)], Geopena ongean me lifes geat. þæt ðæra ðeostra ealdras me ne gemeton. [014100 (215.266)], Ðu eart crist þæs lifiendan godes sunu. þu ðe be þines fæder hæse middaneard gehældest. & us ðone halgan gast gesendest. [014200 (215.268)], Ðe we heriað & þanciað. þinra menigfealdra goda geond ungeendode worulde. amen. [014300 (215.271)], Æfter þysum gebede æteowde heofenlic leoht bufon þam apostole binnon þære byrgyne ane tid: swa beorhte scinende þæt nanes mannes gesihð þæs leohtes leoman sceawian ne mihte & he mid þam leohte his gast ageaf þam drihtne þe hine to his rice gelaðode. [014400 (215.274)], He gewat swa freoh fram deaðes sarnysse of þisum andwerdan life: swa swa he wæs ælfremed fram lichamlicere gewemmednysse. [014500 (215.276)], Soðlice syððan wæs his byrgen gemet: mid manna afylled. [014600 (216.277)], Manna wæs gehaten se heofonlica mete: þe feowertig geara afedde israhela folc on westene. [014700 (216.279)], Nu wæs se bigleofa gemet on iohannes byrgene. & nan þing elles. & se mete is weaxende on hire: oð ðysne andwerdan dæg. [014800 (216.280)], Ðær beoð fela tacna æteowede. & untrume gehælde: & fram eallum frecednyssum alysede. þurh ðæs apostoles þingunge. [014900 (216.282)], Ðæs him getiþað drihten crist: þam is wurðmynt & wuldor: mid fæder & halgum gaste. a buton ende. AMEN.
