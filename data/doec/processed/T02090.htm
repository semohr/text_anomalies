[000300 (217.8)], Ða þa se hælend acenned wæs. on þære iudeiscan bethleem. on herodes dagum cyninges: efne þa comon fram eastdæle middaneardes þry tungelwitegan to þære byrig hierusalem. þus befrinende. [000400 (217.11)], Hwær is iudeiscra leoda cyning. se þe acenned is. [000500 (217.12)], We gesawon soðlice his steorran on eastdæle: & we comon to þy þæt we us to him gebiddan. [000600 (217.13)], Hwæt ða herodes cyning þis gehyrende wearð micclum astyred & eal seo burhwaru samod mid him. [000700 (217.15)], He þa gesamnode ealle ða ealdorbiscopas. & þæs folces boceras. & befran hwær cristes cenningstow wære. [000800 (217.16)], Hi sædon on þære iudeiscan bethleem. [000900 (217.17)], Ðus soðlice is awriten þurh ðone witegan micheam. [001000 (217.17)], Eala þu betleem iudeisc land ne eart ðu nateshwon wacost burga on iudeiscum ealdrum: of þe cymþ se heretoga. se þe gewylt & gewissað israhela folc. [001100 (217.20)], Ða clypode herodes þa ðry tungelwitegan on sunderspræce: & geornlice hi befran. to hwilces timan se steorra him ærest æteowede & sende hi to bethleem þus cweþende. [001200 (217.22)], Farað ardlice & befrinað be þam cilde: & þonne ge hit gemetað cyþað me. þæt ic mæge me to him gebiddan. [001300 (217.24)], Ða tungelwitegan ferdon æfter þæs cinges spræce: & efne þa se steorra þe hi on eastdæle gesawon glad him beforan: oð ðæt he gestod bufon þam gesthuse þær ðæt cild onwunode. [001400 (218.27)], Hi gesawon þone steorran. & þearle blissodon. [001500 (218.27)], Eodon þa in & þæt cild gemetton mid marian his meder: & nyþerfeallende hi to him gebædon. [001600 (218.29)], Hi geopenodon heora hordfatu. & him lac geoffrodon: gold. & recels & mirran. [001700 (218.30)], Hwæt þa god. on swefne hi gewarnode. & bebead þæt hi eft ne gecyrdon to þam reþan cyninge herode: ac þurh oðerne weg hine forcyrdon. & swa to heora eþele becomon. [001800 (218.32)], Efne þa godes engel æteowde iosepe þæs cildes fosterfæder: on swefnum cweðende. [001900 (218.34)], Aris & nim þis cild mid þære meder: & fleoh to egypta lande: & beo þær oð ðæt ic þe eft secge. [002000 (218.35)], Soðlice toweard is þæt herodes smeað hu he ðæt cild fordo: Ioseph þa aras nihtes: & þæt cild mid þære meder samod to egypta lande ferede. & þær wunode oð ðæt herodes gewat: þæt seo witegung wære gefylled. þe be ðære fare ær þus cwæð. [002100 (218.39)], Of egypta lande ic geclypode minne sunu. [002200 (218.40)], Nu secgað wyrdwriteras þæt herodes betwux þysum wearð gewreged to þam romaniscum casere þe ealne middaneard on þam timan geweold. [002300 (218.42)], Ða gewende he to rome be ðæs caseres hæse þæt he hine betealde gif he mihte. [002400 (218.43)], Ða betealde he hine swiðe geaplice swa swa he wæs snotorwyrde. to þan swiðe þæt se casere hine mid maran wurðmynte ongean to iudeiscum rice asende. [002500 (218.45)], Ða ða he ham com þa gemunde he hwæt he ær be þam cilde gemynte. & geseah þæt he wæs bepæht fram þam tungelwitegum. & wearð þa ðearle gegremod. [002600 (218.48)], Sende þa his cwelleras. & ofsloh ealle þa hysecild þe wæron on þære byrig bethleem. & on eallum hyre gemærum. fram twywintrum cilde to anre nihte: be þære tide þe he geaxode æt ðam tungelwitegum. [002700 (218.50)], Ða wæs gefylled hieremias witegung þe ðus witegode. [002800 (218.51)], Stemn is gehyred on heannysse micel wop & þoterung. rachel beweop hire cildru. & nolde beon gefrefrod. for þan ðe hi ne sind. [002900 (218.54)], On þam twelftan dæge cristes acennednysse comon þa þry tungelwitegan to herode & hine axodon be þam acennedan cilde. & þa ða hi his cenningstowe geaxodon þa gewendon hi wið ðæs cildes & noldon þone reðan cwellere eft gecyrran swa swa he het. [003000 (218.57)], Ða ne mihte he forbugan þæs caseres hæse. & wæs þa þurh his langsume fær þæra cildra slege geuferod. swiðor þonne he gemynt hæfde. & hi wurdon þa on þysum dægþerlicum dæge wuldorfullice gemartyrode: na swa þeah þæs geares ðe crist acenned wæs. ac æfter twegra geara ymbrene. æfter þæs wælhreowan hamcyme. [003100 (219.62)], Næs he æþelboren. ne him naht to þam cynecynne ne gebyrode: ac mid syrewungum & swicdome he becom to ðære cynelican geþincþe. swa swa moyses be þam awrat þæt ne sceolde ateorian þæt iudeisce cynecynn: oð ðæt crist sylf come. [003200 (219.66)], Ða com crist on þam timan þe seo cynelice mægð ateorode. & se ælfremeda herodes þæs rices geweold: þa wearþ he micclum afyrht. & anþracode þæt his rice feallan sceolde. þurh tocyme þæs soðan cyninges. [003300 (219.69)], Ða clypode he þa tungelwitegan on sunderspræce. & geornlice hi befran on hwilcne timan hi ærest þone steorran gesawon: for þan ðe he ondred swa swa hit gelamp þæt hi eft hine ne gecyrdon. [003400 (219.72)], Ða het he for þi acwellan ealle þa hysecild þære burhscire fram twywintrum cilde oð anre nihte: þohte gif he hi ealle ofsloge þæt se an ne ætburste þe he sohte. [003500 (219.74)], Ac he wæs ungemyndig þæs halgan gewrites. þe cwyð. [003600 (219.75)], Nis nan wisdom ne nan ræd naht ongean god. [003700 (219.77)], Se swicola herodes cwæð to þam tungelwitegum. farað & geornlice befrinað be þam cilde: & cyþað me þæt ic eac mage me to him gebiddan. [003800 (219.79)], Ac he cydde syððan his facenfullan syrewunge. hu he embe wolde gif he hine gemette: þa ða he ealle his euanealdan adilegode. for his anes ehtnysse. [003900 (219.81)], Ðearfleas he syrwede ymbe crist: ne com he for þi þæt he wolde his eorðlice rice oððe æniges oðres cyninges mid ricetere him to geteon: ac to þy he com þæt he wolde his heofenlice rice geleaffullum mannum forgyfan. [004000 (219.84)], Ne com he to þy þæt he wære on mærlicum cynesetle ahafen: ac ðæt he wære mid hospe on rodehengene genæglod. [004100 (219.86)], He wolde þeah þæs wælhreowan syrewunge mid fleame forbugan: na for ðy þæt he deað forfluge: se þe sylfwilles to þrowienne middanearde genealæhte: ac hit wære to hrædlic gif he þa on cildcradole acweald wurde: swilce þonne his tocyme manncynne bedigelod wære: þy forhradode godes engel þæs arleasan geþeaht. & bebead þæt se fosterfæder þone heofonlican æðeling of þam earde ardlice ferede. [004200 (220.93)], Ne forseah crist his geongan cempan þeah ðe he lichamlice on heora slege andwerd nære: ac he asende hi fram þysum wræcfullum life to his ecan rice.
