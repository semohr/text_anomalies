[011800 (240.248)], Ure eard soðlice is neorcsenawang: to þam we ne magon gecyrran þæs weges þe we comon. [011900 (240.250)], Se frumsceapena man. & eall his ofspring wearð adræfed of neorxenawanges myrhðe þurh ungehyrsumnysse. & for þigene þæs forbodenan bigleofan & þurh modignysse. þa ða he wolde beon betera þonne hine se ælmihtiga scyppend gesceop. [012000 (240.253)], Ac us is micel neod þæt we þurh oðerne weg þone swicolan deofol forbugon: þæt we moton gesælilice to urum eðle becuman: þe we to gesceapene wæron. [012100 (240.255)], We sceolon. þurh gehyrsumnysse & forhæfednysse & eadmodnysse. anmodlice to urum eþele stæppan: & mid halgum mægnum þone eard ofgan. þe we þurh leahtrum forluron. [012200 (240.258)], Rihtlice wæs se swicola herodes fram þam tungelwitegum bepæht. & he to criste ne becom: for þan ðe he mid facenfullum mode hine sohte. [012300 (240.260)], He getacnode þa leasan licceteras. þe mid hiwunge god secað & næfre ne gemetað. [012400 (240.261)], He is to secenne mid soðfæstre heortan. & anrædum mode: se þe leofað & rixað. mid fæder & halgum gaste. on ealra worulda woruld. AMEN.
