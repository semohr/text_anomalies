[011900 (256.231)], Micel gifu wæs þæt. ðære mennyscnysse. þæt he wæs godes sunu & god sylf: swa hraðe swa he ongann. man to beonne. [012000 (256.232)], He wæs æfre god. of þam fæder acenned & wuniende mid þam fæder. & mid þam halgan gaste: hi ðry an god untodæledlic: þry on hadum & an god. on anre godcundnesse. & on anum gecynde. æfre wuniende. [012100 (256.235)], Se sunu ana underfeng þa menniscnesse. & hæfde annginn. se ðe æfre wæs. [012200 (256.236)], He wæs cyld. & weox on ðære mennyscnysse. [012300 (256.237)], And þrowade deað sylfwilles: & aras of deaðe mid þam lichaman þe he ær on ðrowade: & astah to heofenum: & wunað nu æfre on godcundnysse: & on menniscnysse. an crist æigðer ge god ge mann: undeadlic. se ðe ær his þrowunge. wæs deadlic. [012400 (256.241)], Æne he ðrowade: Ac he ne þrowað heononforð. næfre eft: Ac bið æfre butan ende ealswa [[../images/etail.gif]]ce: on ðære mennyscnysse swa he is. on ðære godcundnysse. [012500 (256.244)], Wite gehwa eac. þæt gesett is on cyriclicum ðeawum þæt we sceolon on ðisum dæge beran ure leoht to cyrican & lætan hi ðær bletsian: & we scolon gan syððan mid þam leohte betwux godes husum & singan þone lofsang. þe ðær to geset is þeah ðe sume men singan ne cunnon hi beron þeahhwæðere þæt leoht on hyra handum: for ðy on ðysum dæge. wæs þæt soðe leoht crist. geboren to ðæm temple: Se ðe us alysde fram þeostrum & us gebrincð to ðam ecan leohte. [012600 (257.250)], Se ðe lyfað & rixað a butan ende. AMEN.
