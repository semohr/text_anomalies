[014300 (343.228)], He wolde don crist læssan þonne he is. & his godcundnysse wurðmynt wanian. þa wearð him swa bysmorlic deað geseald swa swa he wel wyrðe wæs. [014400 (343.231)], Oðer gedwolman wæs se hatte sabellius. he cwæð þæt se fæder wære. þa ða he wolde fæder & eft þa ða he wolde he wære sunu. & eft þa ða he wolde wære halig gast. & wære for ði an god. [014500 (343.233)], Ða forwearð eac þes gedwola mid his gedwylde. [014600 (343.235)], Nu eft þæt iudeisce folc þe crist ofslogon swa swa he sylf wolde. & geþafode: secgað ðæt hi wyllað gelyfan on þone fæder & na on þone sunu þe heora magas ofslogon. [014700 (343.237)], Heora geleafa is naht. & hi for þi losiað. [014800 (343.238)], For ure alysednysse crist geþafode þæt hi hine ofslogon. [014900 (343.238)], Hit ne mihte eall mancyn gedon gif he sylf nolde. [015000 (343.239)], Ac se halga fæder gesceop & geworhte mancyn þurh his sunu. & he wolde eft þurh ðone ylcan us alysan fram hellewite. þa ða we forwyrhte wæron. [015100 (343.241)], Buton ælcere þrowunge he mihte us habban ac him þuhte þæt unrihtlic. [015200 (343.243)], Ac se deofol forwyrhte hine sylfne þa ða he tihte þæt iudeisce folc to þæs hælendes slege & we wurdon alysede þurh his unscyldigum deaðe fram þam ecan deaðe. [015300 (343.246)], We habbað þone geleafan þe crist sylf tæhte his apostolum. & hi eallum mancynne: & þone geleafan god hæfð mid manegum wundrum getrymmed & gefæstnod. [015400 (343.248)], Ærest crist þurh hine sylfne dumbe & deafe. healte & blinde. wode & hreoflige gehælde. & þa deadan to life arærde. & syððan þurh his apostolas & oþre halige men ðas ylcan wundra geworhte. nu eac on urum timan gehwær ðær halige men hi restað. æt heora deadum banum god wyrcð fela wundra to ði ðæt he wile folces geleafan mid þam wundrum getrymman. [015500 (344.253)], Ne wyrcð god na þas wundra æt nanes iudeisces mannes byrgene ne æt nanes oðres gedwolan. ac æt rihtgelyfedra manna byrgenum þa ðe gelyfdon on þære halgan þrynnysse. & on soþre annysse anre godcundnysse. [015600 (344.257)], Wite gehwa eac þæt nan man ne mot beon tua gefullod. ac gif se man æfter his fulluhte aslide we gelyfað þæt he mage beon gehealden gif he his synna mid wope behreowsað. & be lareowa tæcunge hi gebet. [015700 (344.260)], We sceolon gelyfan þæt ælces mannes sawul bið þurh god gesceapen ac hwæðere heo ne bið na of godes agenum gecynde. [015800 (344.261)], Ðæs mannes lichaman antimber bið of þam fæder & of þære meder: ac god gescypð þone lichaman of þam antimbre & asent on þam lichaman sawle. [015900 (344.264)], Ne bið seo sawul nahwar wuniende æror ac god hi gescypð þærrihte. & beset on þam lichaman & læt hi habban agenne cyre swa heo syngie swa heo synna forbuge. [016000 (344.266)], Ðeahhwæðere heo behofað æfre godes fultumes. þæt heo mage synna forbugan. & eft to hyre scyppende gecuman þurh godum geearnungum. for þan ðe nan man ne deð buton gode nan þing to gode. [016100 (344.270)], Eac we sceolon gelyfan þæt ælc lichama þe sawle underfeng sceal arisan on domesdæge mid þam ylcan lichaman þe he nu hæfð. & sceal onfon edlean ealra his dæda. þonne habbað þa godan ece lif mid gode & he sylð þa mede ælcum be his geearnungum. [016200 (344.273)], Ða synfullan beoð on hellewite. a. þrowigende & heora wite bið eac gemetegod ælcum be his geearnungum. [016300 (344.275)], Uton for þi geearnian þæt ece lif mid gode þurh ðisne geleafan & þurh godum geearnungum. se ðe þurhwunað on þrynnysse. an ælmihtig god. a. on ecnysse. AMEN.
