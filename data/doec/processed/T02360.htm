[012800 (409.248)], Is nu for þy munuchades mannum mid micelre gecnyrdnysse to forbugenne þas yfelan gebysnunga: & geeuenlæcan þam apostolum. þæt hi mid him & mid gode þæt ece lif habban moton. AMEN.
