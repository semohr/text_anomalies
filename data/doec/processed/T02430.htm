[012200 (473.242)], Mid þysum wordum is geswutelod þæt ælcum geleaffullum menn is engel to hyrde geset þe hine wið deofles syrewungum gescylt. & on halgum mægnum gefultumað. swa swa se sealmscop be gehwylcum rihtwisum cwæð. god bebead his englum. be ðe. þæt hi ðe healdon & on heora handum hebbon þy læs þe þu æt stane þinne fot ætspurne. [012300 (474.247)], Micel wurðscipe cristenra manna þæt gehwylc hæbbe fram his acennednysse him betæhtne engel to hyrdrædene swa swa be þam apostole petre awriten is þa ða se engel hine of þam cwearterne gelædde. & he to his geferum becom. & cnuciende inganges bæd. þa cwædon þa geleaffullan nis hit na petrus þæt ðær cnucað. ac is his engel. [012400 (474.252)], Ða englas soðlice ðe god gesette to hyrdum his gecorenum hi ne gewitað næfre fram his andwerdnysse: for þan ðe god is æighwar. & swa hweder swa þa englas fleoð. æfre hi beoð binnon his andwerdnysse. & his wuldres brucað. [012500 (474.255)], Hi bodiað ure weorc & gebedu þam ælmihtigum þeah ðe him nan þing digle ne sy. swa swa se heahengel raphahel cwæð to þam godes menn tobian. þa þa ge eow gebædon. ic offrode eowere gebedu ætforan gode. [012600 (474.259)], Seo ealde æ. segð us þæt heahenglas sind gesette ofer gehwylcum leodscipum þæt hi þæs folces gyman ofer þam oþrum englum: swa swa moyses on þære fiftan bec þære ealdan æ þysum wordum geswutelode. [012700 (474.262)], Ða ða se healica god todælde. & tostencte adames ofspring. þa sette he ðeoda gemæru. æfter getele his engla. [012800 (474.263)], Ðysum andgite geþwærlæhð. se witega danihel on his witegunge. [012900 (474.264)], Sum godes engel spræc to danihele. embe þone heahengel. þe perscisce þeode bewiste & cwæð. [013000 (474.266)], Me com to se heahengel greciscre þeode ealdor: and nis heora nan min gefylsta buton michael ebreisces folces ealdor. [013100 (474.268)], Efne nu michahel an þæra fyrmestra ealdra com me to fultume and ic wunode þær wið ðone cyning perscisre þeode. [013200 (474.269)], Mid þysum wordum is geswutelod hu micele care þa heahenglas habbað heora ealdordomes ofer mancynne þa ða he cwæð. þæt michahel him come to fultume. [013300 (474.272)], Is nu geleaflic þæt se heahengel michahel hæbbe gymene cristenra manna: se þe wæs ebreiscan folces ealdor. þa hwile ðe hi on god belyfdon & þæt he geswutelode þa ða he him sylfum cyrcan getimbrode betwux geleaffulre þeode on þam munte gargano swa swa we hwene ær ræddon. [013400 (475.276)], Ðæt is gedon be godes fadunge þæt se mæra heofonlica engel beo singallice cristenra manna gefylsta on eorþan & þingere on heofenum to þam ælmihtigum gode: se þe leofað & rixað a on ecnysse AMEN.
