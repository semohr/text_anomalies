id,text [000100 (531.1)], Hit wære gelimplic gif þises dæges scortnys us geþafian wolde þæt we eow þæs halgan apostoles andrees þrowunge gerehton: ac we wyllað on oðrum sæle gif we gesundfulle beoð eow gelæstan gif we hwæt lytles hwonlicor gefyldon. [000200 (532.4)], Uton nu eadmodlice gebiddan þæs ælmihtigan godes mægenþrym þæt his eadiga apostol andreas beo ure ece þingere to him. swa swa he wæs soð bydel his halgan gelaþunge. [000300 (532.6)], Sy wuldor & lof þam ælmihtigan scyppende a on ecnysse. AMEN.
