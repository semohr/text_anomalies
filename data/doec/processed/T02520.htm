[013300 (10.267)], Crist wolde þæt manega witegan. and eac ða hæðenan sceoldon bodian his tocyme. and cyðan his fær þæt mancynn wære þæs ðe geleaffulre. and ðæs þe gewisre on hwæne hi sceoldon gelyfan. and ealle cweðan ægðer ge mid muðe ge mid mode. swa se sealmscop sang be gode; [013400 (10.271)], Þu eart mære and micel ðe wundra wyrcst. þu eart ana god; [013500 (10.272)], We sceolon ægðer gelyfan godes wundra. and eac mid micelre lufe geðancian þam heofonlican fæder gode ælmihtigum. þæt he wolde asendan his ancennedan sunu to ðysum life for ure alysednysse. ða ða we forwyrhte wæron; [013600 (10.277)], We sceolon eac cristes acennednysse. and his gebyrdtide mid gastlicere blisse wurðian. and us sylfe mid godum weorcum geglengan. and us mid godes lofsangum gebysgian. and ða ðing onscunian. ðe crist forbytt. þæt sind leahtras. and deofles weorc. and ða ðing lufian ðe god bebead. þæt is eadmodnys. and mildheortnys. rihtwisnys. and soðfæstnys. ælmesdæda. and gemetfæstnys. geþyld and clænnyss; [013700 (11.283)], Þas ðing lufað god and huru ða clænnysse ðe he sylf ðurh hine. and ðurh þæt clæne mæden his modor astealde; [013800 (11.285)], Swa eac ealle his geferan ðe him filigdon ealle hi wæron on clænnysse wuniende. and se mæsta dæl þæra manna þe gode geðeoð þurh clænnysse hi geðeoð; [013900 (11.288)], Warniað eow wið oferfylle and oferdrence. swa swa crist cwæð on his godspelle; [014000 (11.289)], Beoð wære þæt eowere heortan ne beon gehefgode mid oferfylle. and druncennysse. and mid woruldcarum. and se færlica deað becume ofer eow; [014100 (11.292)], Uton beon eac gemyndige hu micelre geðincðe sy þæt halige mæden Maria cristes moder; [014200 (11.293)], Heo is gebletsod ofer eallum wifhades mannum. heo is seo heofenlice cwen. and ealra cristenra manna frofer and fultum; [014300 (11.295)], Ure ealde moder Eua us beleac heofenan rices geat. and seo halige Maria hit eft us geopenode. gif we hit sylfe nu mid yfelum weorcum us ne belucað; [014400 (11.298)], Micel mæg heo æt hire bearne abiddan. gif heo bið geornlice to gemynegod; [014500 (11.299)], Uton for ði mid micelre geornfulnysse hi gebiddan. þæt heo us ðingige to hire agenum bearne. se ðe is ægðer ge hire scyppend. ge hire sunu. soð god and soð mann an crist. se ðe leofað and rixað mid fæder. and mid halgum gaste. hi ðry an god a. on ecnysse;
