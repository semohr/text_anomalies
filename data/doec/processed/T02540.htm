[002800 (20.50)], Þa cwæð Iohannes to ðam folce; [002900 (20.50)], Ne eom ic na crist. ac ic fullige eow on wætere to ði. þæt ge sceolon eowre synna behreowsian; [003000 (20.52)], Æfter me cymð se ðe is strengra þonne ic. and ic ne eom na wyrðe þæt ic mote furðon his sceoðwang unbindan. he fullað eow on ðam halgan gaste. and on fyre; [003100 (20.55)], Þas word he clypode be criste for ði. þe cristes fulluht ðwehð þone man ægðer ge utan ge innan; [003200 (20.56)], Þæt wæter wiðutan. and se halga gast wiðinnan; [003300 (20.57)], Iohannes fulluht ðwoh þone mannan wiðutan. and nan ðing wiðinnan. for ði ðe he ne sealde nane synne forgifenysse. swa swa Crist dyde þurh ðone halgan gast; [003400 (21.61)], Þa ða Crist wæs ðritig wintra. þa com he on ðisum dæge to Iohannes fulluhte æt ðære ea þe is gehaten Iordanis. and wolde beon gefullod æt his handum; [003500 (21.63)], Ða ða Iohannes hine geseah cumende to him. þa cwæð he be him; [003900 (21.67)], La leof ic sceal beon gefullod æt ðinum handum. and þu cymst to minum fulluhte; [004000 (21.69)], Crist ða him geandwyrde; [004100 (21.69)], Læt nu ðus. and geðafa ðis. swa unc gedafenað þæt wit gefyllon ealle rihtwisnysse; [004800 (21.85)], Þis nis nu alyfed nanum men to donne. for ðan ðe þæs lambes slege getacnode cristes slege; [004900 (21.86)], He ne wiðerode ongean. ne ne feaht þe swiðor þe lamb deð. ac geðafode swiðe geðyldelice þæt he wære geoffrod for ealles middaneardes synnum; [005000 (21.88)], For ði buton he ðrowode for us. ne mihte ure nan cuman to godes rice; [005100 (21.91)], Crist wolde beon gefullod na for ði ðe him neod wære æniges fulluhtes. for ði ðe he næfre nane synne ne geworhte. ac he wolde mid his eadmodnysse astellan ða bysne. þæt nan cyning ne nan rice man ne sceolde þincan to huxlic þæt he gebuge to Cristes fulluhte. þa ða he sylf gemedemode þæt he wolde gebigan his halige heafod to his ðeowan handum; [005200 (22.96)], Ða ða he into ðam wætere eode. ða wæs þæt wæter and ealle wyllspringas gehalgode þurh cristes lichaman to urum fulluhte; [005300 (22.98)], Micel wæs cristes eadmodnys. ða ða he com sylf to ðam fulluhtere. and micel wæs Iohannes eadmodnys. ða ða he ne dorste crist gefullian. ær ðan ðe he gehaten wæs; [005400 (22.101)], Ac for ði þe nan eadmodnys nis fulfremed. buton hire gefera beo gehyrsumnys. þa gefylde he eadmodlice þæt þæt he ær wiðsoc forhtigende; [005500 (22.103)], Se hælend cwæð; [005600 (22.104)], Geðafa þæt ic beo gefullod æt ðinum handum on wætere. and ðu siððan swa swa ðu gewilnast beo æt minum handum gefullod þurh ðone halgan gast; We rædað on ðam ealdan gesetnyssum. þæt ða halgan lareowas tæhton þone soðan geleafan þam mannum þe to cristendome gebugon. and axodon hi hwæðer hi woldon wiðsacan deofle. and on god gelyfan; [012100 (26.250)], Hi beheton þæt hi woldon swa don. and wurdon ða gefullode on halgum fante. mid þam behate; [012200 (26.252)], Þa unsprecendan cild hi fullodon ðurh geleafan þæs fæder. and ðære meder. and se godfæder wæs þæs cildes forspreca. and borh wið god þæt hit heolde þone cristendom be godes tæcunge. for ðan ðe se cwyde is swiðe egeslic þe crist cwæð. þæt nan ungefullod mann ne becymð to ðam ecan life; [012300 (26.257)], Nu stent ðeos gesetnys on godes gelaðunge. þæt man ða unsprecendan cild fullige. and hi beoð gehealdene þurh oðra manna geleafan. swa swa hi wæron þurh oðra manna synna geniðerade. for ðan ðe hit bið twylic hwæðer hit on life aðolige. oð þæt hit þam lareowe mid geleafan andwyrdan mage; [012400 (27.262)], We habbað full swutele bysne þises ðinges; [012500 (27.262)], Sum wif wæs ðe com to criste. and bæd for hire dehter þe læg on wodum dreame; [012600 (27.264)], Þa cwæð crist to hire þæt hit nære na rihtlic þæt man name his cildra hlaf. and wurpe hundum; [012700 (27.265)], Heo ða andwyrde; [012800 (27.265)], Gea leof drihten. and þeah hwæðere oft ða hwelpas gelæccað þa cruman þe feallað of þæs hlafordes beode; [012900 (27.267)], Ða andwyrde se hælend. and cwæð; [013000 (27.268)], Eala ðu wif. micel is ðin geleafa. Getimige ðe swa swa ðu wilt; [013100 (27.269)], Hire dohtor wearð þærrihte gewittig; [013200 (27.269)], Þa wæs seo dohtor gehæled þurh geleafan ðære meder; [013300 (27.270)], Swa beoð eac ða ungewittigan cild gehealdene on ðam fulluhte þurh geleafan ðæs fæder. and ðære meder. and ðurh forespræce þæs godfæder; [013400 (27.273)], Se mæssepreost axað þæt cild. and cweð; [013500 (27.273)], Wiðsæcst ðu deofle? ðonne andwyrt se godfæder þæs cildes wordum and cweð; [013600 (27.275)], Ic wiðsace deofle; [013700 (27.275)], Þonne axað he eft; [013800 (27.275)], Wiðsæcst ðu eallum his weorcum? He cweð ic wiðsace; [013900 (27.276)], He axað þriddan siðe; [014000 (27.277)], Wiðsæcst ðu eallum his getotum? He cwyð ic wiðsace; [014100 (27.278)], Þonne hæfð he wiðsacen on ðisum ðrym wordum deofle. and eallum leahtrum; [014200 (27.279)], Ðonne axað he gyt; [014300 (27.279)], Gelyfst ðu on ðære halgan ðrynnysse. and soðre annysse? He andwyrt ic gelyfe; [014400 (27.281)], Se godes ðen befrinð þonne gyt; [014500 (27.281)], Gelyfst ðu þæt we sceolon ealle arisan mid urum lichaman on domes dæge togeanes criste. and þæt ðær gehwa onfo edlean ealra his weorca. swa swa he ær on life geearnode? He andwyrt ic gelyfe; [014600 (27.284)], And se preost gefullað þæt cild mid þisum geleafan; [014700 (27.285)], Hit wexð and gæð forð. and ne cann þyses geleafan nan ðing; [014800 (27.287)], Is nu for ði micel neod gehwam þæt he leornige æt his lareowe hu he his cristendom healdan sceole. mid þam soðan geleafan. and hu he mage deofol forbugan. and helle wite. and geearnian þæt ece lif. and ða ecan myrhðe mid gode. for ðan ðe se apostol Paulus spræc swiðe egeslice be ungelyfedum mannum; [014900 (27.291)], He cwæð; [015000 (27.292)], Þa ðe godes .æ. ne cunnon. and buton godes .æ. syngiað. hi eac buton godes .æ. losiað; [015100 (27.293)], And eft; [015200 (27.293)], Se mann þe god forgyt. God forgyt eac hine; [015300 (28.294)], Uton don for ði swa swa se ylca apostol tæhte. Genealæcað to gode. and god genealæhð to eow; [015400 (28.295)], And se sealmscop us mynegað eft ðus cweðende; [015500 (28.296)], Eadig bið se wer se ðe hine ondræt god. and awent his willan to his bebodum; [015600 (28.297)], To ðyssere eadignysse and to ðære ecan eadignysse gebringe us se ælmihtiga god. se ðe leofað and rixað a buton ende.
