Se halga abbud. on ðisum andwerdum dæge gewat of ðisum deadlicum life. to ðam ecan ðe he ær deoplice mid haligre drohtnunge geearnode; [000200 (92.3)], He wæs of eawfæstum magum æðellice geboren. and hi hine on cildhade to lare befæston on romebyrig. gelæredum uðwitum; [000400 (92.11)], Ða abæd his fostermoder an hridder. and hit tobærst on emtwa on ðære læne; [000500 (92.12)], Seo fostormodor ða sarlice weop for ðære awyrdan læne. ac se eawfæsta Benedictus besargode his fostormoder sarnysse swiðe arfæstlice. and genam ða sticcu þæs toclofenan hriddores. and mid wope on his gebedum cneowode; [000600 (92.16)], Ac ða ða he of ðam gebedum aras. ða gemette he þæt fæt wið hine licgende. swa gehal þæt ðær nan cinu on næs gesewen; [000700 (92.18)], Þa wearð þis wundor on ðære stowe sona gewidmærsod. and hi for wundrunge þæt hridder upahengon æt heora cyrcan geate. þæt men mihton tocnawan þæs mæran Bendictes mærða. hwylce geðincðe he hæfde ætforan gode ða giu on his cildhade; [021200 (108.554)], Þæs geares ðe he gewat. he cyðde his forðsið on ær. sumum his leorningcnihtum mid him drohtnigendum. and sumum oðrum on fyrlenum stowum wunigendum; [021300 (108.556)], Seofon nihtum ær he gewite he het his byrgene geopenian. and he ðærrihte mid stiðlicum fefore geond ða seofon niht þearle gedreht wearð; [021400 (108.559)], On ðam sixtan dæge his legeres he het hine beran into cyrcan. and þær hine gehuslian; [021500 (108.560)], He ða astod betwux his gebroðra handum. astrehtum handum wið heofonas weard. and betwux his gebedum his gast ut ableow; [021600 (108.562)], On ðam ylcan dæge wearð æteowod his twam leorningcnihtum an weg. fram ðam huse þe he on gewat on ðam eastdæle. astreht oð heofonan; [021700 (108.564)], Se weg wæs mid pællum gebricgod. and mid ungerimum leohtfatum scinende; [021800 (108.566)], Ðær on uppon stod sum arwurðe wer mid beorhtum gyrlum. axigende hwæs se weg wære. þe hi beheoldon; [021900 (108.567)], Hi cwædon þæt hi nyston; [022000 (108.568)], Þa cwæð se engel him to; [022100 (108.568)], Ðis is se weg ðe godes dyrling Benedictus to heofenum on astah; [022200 (108.570)], His halga lichama wearð ða bebyriged to his sweoster lice Scolastican. swa swa he sylf bebead binnon Iohannes cyrcan þæs halgan fulluhteres. on ðam munte Casino. ac he wæs siððan æfter manegum gearum geferod to francena rice. to þam mynstre ðe we hatað Florege. on ðære stowe his ban restað on micclum wurðmynte. and on wundrum scinende. and his sawl symle gesælig rixað mid gode on heofenum. for godum geearnungum; [022300 (109.577)], Þæt scræf ðe he ærest on drohtnigende wæs. gyt oð ðis on wundrum scinende ðurhwunað; [022400 (109.578)], Witodlice sum gemyndleas wif ferde worigende geond wudas. and feldas. and ðær gelæg þær hi seo teorung gelette; [022500 (109.580)], Ða beeode heo sume dæge þurh nytennysse into ðam scræfe þæs eadigan benedictes. and þær hi gereste. and aras ðæs on merigen swa gewittig. swilce heo næfre on nanre wodnysse nære. and swa siððan symle ðurhwunode; [022600 (109.585)], Hwa mæg on worulde ealle ða wundra gereccan. ðe se ælmihtiga scyppend ðurh ðisne æðelan wer middanearde geswutelode; [022700 (109.587)], Sy him wuldor and lof.
