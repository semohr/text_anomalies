[013800 (135.262)], Þurh næddran us becom deað. and for ði wæs þurh ðære næddran gelicnysse cristes deað getacnod; [013900 (135.264)], Ðæra nædrena geslit wæs deadlic. Cristes deað wæs liflic; [014000 (135.265)], Nu behealde we ða næddran. þæt seo næddre us ne derige; [014100 (135.266)], Hwæt gemænð þæt? We behealdað cristes deað. þæt us se deað ne derige. þe of ðære næddran asprang. seo ðe Adam forspeon; [014200 (135.268)], Hwæs deað behealde we? Lifes deað; [014300 (135.269)], Hwa is lif buton crist? Se ðe cwæð. Ic eom ærist. and lif. se ðe gelyfð on me. þeah ðe he dead beo. he leofað. and ælc ðæra þe leofað and on me gelyfð. ne swelt he on ecnysse; [014400 (136.272)], Crist is lif. and swa ðeah he wæs on rode ahangen; [014500 (136.273)], He is soð lif. and swa ðeah he wæs dead on ðære menniscnysse. na on godcundnysse; [014600 (136.274)], On cristes deaðe wæs se deað adydd. for ðan þe þæt deade lif acwealde ðone deað. and he wæs fornumen on cristes lichaman; [014700 (136.277)], Mine gebroðru uton behealdan þone ahangenan crist. þæt we beon fram ðam ættrigum synnum gehælede; [014800 (136.278)], Witodlice swa swa þæt israhela folc besawon to ðære ærenan næddran. and wurdon gehælede fram ðæra næddrena geslite. swa beoð nu gehælede fram heora synnum þa ðe mid geleafan behealdað cristes deað. and his ærist; [014900 (136.282)], Hi wæron gehælede fram deaðe to hwilwendlicum life. and her is gecweden þæt we sceolon habban þæt ece lif; [015000 (136.284)], Swa micel is betwux þære gehiwodan anlicnysse. and ðam soðan ðinge; [015100 (136.285)], Seo gehiwode anlicnys getiðode þam toslitenum mannum hwilwendlic lif. þæt soðe ðing þe ðurh ða ærenan næddran getacnod wæs. þæt is cristes deað getiðað us þæt ece lif; [015200 (136.288)], Þurh treow us com deað. þa ða adam geæt þone forbodenan æppel. and ðurh treow us com eft lif. and alysednyss. ða ða crist hangode on rode for ure alysednysse; [015300 (136.290)], Ðære halgan rode tacn. is ure bletsung. and to ðære rode we us gebiddað. na swa ðeah to ðam treowe. ac to ðam ælmihtigum drihtne. ðe on ðære halgan rode for us hangode.
