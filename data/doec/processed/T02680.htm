[011500 (158.273)], Næs þæt israhela folc gewunod to hreawum flæsce. þeah ðe god him bebude þæt hi hit hreaw ne æton. ne on wætere gesoden. ac gebræd to fyre; [011600 (158.275)], Se wile ðicgan godes lichaman hreawne. se ðe buton gesceade wenð þæt he wære anfeald man us gelic. and nære god; [011700 (158.277)], And se ðe æfter menniscum wisdome wile smeagan ymbe ða gerynu cristes flæsclicnysse. he deð swilce he seoðe þæs lambes flæsc on wætere. for ðan ðe wæter getacnað on þyssere stowe mennisc ingehyd; [011800 (158.280)], Ac we sceolon witan þæt ealle ða gerynu cristes menniscnysse wæron gefadode þurh mihte þæs halgan gastes. þonne ðicge we his lichaman gebrædne to fyre. for ðan ðe se halga gast com on fyres hiwe to ðam apostolum. on mislicum gereordum; [011900 (158.284)], Israhel sceolde etan þæs lambes heafod. and ða fet. and þæt innewerde. and þær nan ðing belifan ne moste ofer niht. gif þær hwæt belife. forbærnan þæt on fyre. and ne tobræcon ða ban; [012000 (158.287)], Æfter gastlicum andgite we etað þæs lambes heafod. þonne we underfoð cristes godcundnysse on urum geleafan; [012100 (158.289)], Eft ðonne we his menniscnysse mid lufe underfoð. þonne ete we þæs lambes fet. for ðan ðe crist is angynn and ende. God ær ealle worulda. and man on þyssere worulde geendunge; [012200 (158.292)], Hwæt is þæs lambes innewerde buton cristes digelan bebodu. ða we etað þonne we lifes word mid grædignysse underfoð; [012300 (159.294)], Nan ðing ne moste þæs lambes belifan oð merien. for ðan þe godes cwydas sind to smeagenne mid swa micelre carfulnysse. swa þæt ealle his beboda mid andgite. and weorce beon asmeade on nihte ðises andwerdan lifes. ær ðan þe se endenexta dæg. þæs gemænelican æristes æteowige; [012400 (159.298)], Gif we ðonne ealle ða gerynu cristes flæsclicnysse ðurhsmeagan ne magon. ðonne sceole we þa lafe betæcan þæs halgan gastes mihte mid soðre eadmodnysse. and na to dyrstelice embe ða deopan digelnyssa ofer ures andgites mæðe smeagan; [012500 (159.303)], Hi æton þæt lamb mid begyrdum lendenum; [012600 (159.303)], On lendenum is seo galnys ðæs lichaman. and se ðe wile þæt husel ðicgan he sceal gewriðan þa galnysse. and mid clænnysse ða halgan ðigene onfon; [012700 (159.306)], Hi wæron eac gesceode; [012800 (159.306)], Hwæt sind gescy. buton deadra nytena hyda; [012900 (159.307)], We beoð soðlice gesceode. gif we geefenlæcað mid urum færelde and weorce forðfarenra manna lif. þæra ðe gode geðugon þurh gehealtsumnysse his beboda; [013000 (159.309)], Hi hæfdon him stæf on handa æt ðære ðigene. se stæf getacnað gymene. and hyrdrædene; [013100 (159.311)], Þa ðe bet cunnon and magon. sceolon gyman oðra manna. and mid heora fultume underwryðian; [013200 (159.313)], Ðam gemettum wæs beboden þæt hi sceoldon caflice etan. for ðan ðe god onscunað þa sleacnysse on his ðegnum. and ða he lufað þe mid godes cafnysse þæs ecan lifes myrhðe secað; [013300 (159.316)], Hit is awriten. ne elca ðu to gecyrrenne to gode. ðy læs þe se tima losige. þurh ða sleacan elcunge; [013400 (159.317)], Þa gemettan ne moston þæs lambes ban scænan. ne ða cempan ðe crist ahengon ne moston tobrecan his halgan sceancan. swa swa hi dydon þæra twegra sceaðena. ðe him on twa healfa hangodon. Ac drihten aras of deaðe gesund. buton ælcere forrotodnysse. and hi sceolon geseon æt ðam micclan dome hwæne hi gewundodon. wælhreawlice on rode; [013500 (159.324)], Þeos tid is gehaten on ebreiscum gereordepascha. þæt is on ledentransitus. and on englisc færeld. for ðan ðe on ðisum dæge ferde godes folc fram egypta lande ofer ða readan sæ. fram ðeowte to ðam behatenan earde; [013600 (159.327)], Ure drihten ferde eac on þisne timan swa swa se godspellere Iohannes cwæð. fram ðisum middanearde. to his heofonlican fæder; [013700 (160.329)], We sceolon fyligan urum heafde. and faran fram deofle to criste. fram ðissere unstæððigan worulde. to his staðelfæstan rice. ac we sceolon ærest on urum andwerdan life faran fram leahtrum to halgum mægnum. fram unðeawum to godum ðeawum. gif we willað æfter ðisum lænan life. faran to ðam ecan. and æfter urum æriste to hælende criste; [013800 (160.335)], He us gelæde to his lifigendan fæder. þe hine sealde for urum synnum to deaðe; [013900 (160.336)], Sy him wuldor and lof. þære weldæde. on ealra worulda woruld; [014000 (160.337)], Amen:
