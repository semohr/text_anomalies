[013300 (287.264)], Seo cyrce wæs eahtahyrnede. an hund fota and twentig fota heah. six hund fota. and feowertig.heo wæs ymbeganges. eal of fiðerscitum marmstanum geworht; [013400 (287.266)], Ealle ðas getimbrunge geendode se cyning xerxes binnon ðrim gearum. and beworhte ða bigelsas mid gyldenum læfrum. and worhte ane ðruh on hwitum seolfre to þæra apostola lice. and gesette hi on middan þam temple mid micelre arwurðnysse; [013500 (287.271)], On ðisum dæge wearð seo cyrce gehalgod. þam godes apostolum to wurðmynte; [013600 (287.272)], On ðære stowe begytað þa ðe on god gelyfað his weldæda þurh ðæra apostola ðingunge þe on ðisum dæge for his naman ðrowodon; [013700 (287.275)], Þas race awrat se biscop Abdias. se ðe þam apostolum folgode fram Iudea lande; [013800 (287.276)], He awrat hi on ebreiscum gereorde. and his leorningcniht eutropus hi awende eft on greciscum gereorde. and africanus hi awrat eft on tyn bocum. ac us genihtsumað on urum gereorde þas scortan race to getrymminge urum geleafan; [013900 (287.280)], Uton nu biddan þas eadigan apostolas Simon and Iudan. þæt hi us abiddon godes miltsunge. se ðe leofað and rixað on ealra worulda woruld. amen:
