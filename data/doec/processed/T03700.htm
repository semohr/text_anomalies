[012000 (351)]," He cwæð þa mid ileafan: Ic ilyfe, Drihten, and feol to his fotum, for þan ðe he oncneow ðet hit idafenlice wæs, ðæt he to his Drihtne mid astreahtum limum and geleafan hine gebede." [012100 (355)]," Ða cwæð ðe Hælend him eft to þus: Ic com hider on dome on þisne middaneard, þæt ða men iseon þe ne mihten ær iseon, and þa ðe iseoð sceolon beon blinde." [012200 (359)]," Þes mon wæs blind iboren, ac he iseah þurh Criste, ægðer mid eagan and mid geleafan." [012300 (361)]," Þa Iudeiscan wendon þæt heo weron hale, ac heo ne isegon na Crist mid soðæ leafæ, ne his lihtes ne bedon, for þan ðe heo blinde weron."
