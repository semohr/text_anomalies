[],"[000300 (4.124.5)] Ne sceal man manslean, ne unriht hæman, ne stelan, ne unalyfedlice gelustfullian, ne leasunga secgan, ac ælcene man man sceal arwurþian and ne sceal nan man don oðrum, þæt he nelle, þæt him man do." [],"[000400 (4.124.8)] Man sceal hys agenum lustum wiðsacun and folgian Cristes lare, his lichaman gehwa sceal clænsian, þæt ys mid forhæfednyssed wyllan; estmyttas na to grædiglice man ne sceal lufian, ac fæsten man sceal lufian." [],[000500 (4.124.11)] Earmne man sceal hyrtan and nacode scrydan; untrume neosian and deade bebyrgean; geswectum helpan and sarigende frefrian. [],[000600 (4.124.14)] And fram woruldwilnungum hine sceal gehwa fremdian and nan þinc beforan Cristes lufe settan. [],[000700 (4.125.15)] Yrre ne sceal man fulfremman; yrsunge tide ne sceal man gehealdan; facn ne sceal man on heortan habban; lease sibbe ne sceal man syllan; soðe lufe ne sceal man forlætan. [],"[000800 (4.125.18)] Swerigean ne seal man, þe lys man forswerige; soðfæstnysse of heortan and of muðe man sceal symle forðbringan." [],"[000900 (4.125.19)] Ne sceal man yfel mid yfele gyldan, ne nanum men nænne teone ne don, ac þone þe him man yfel do, he sceal geþyldelice hit aræfnian; his find man sceal lufian for Godes lufan; þa wyrgendan ne sceal man na ongeanwyrgean, ac me bletsian; ehtnysse for rihte man sceal eadmodlice þolian." [],"[001000 (4.125.24)] Ne sceal man beon ofermod, ne to druncengeorn, ne foræte, ne toslapol, ne to slaw, ne to grorniende, ne to tælende." [],"[001100 (4.125.25)] Ac ealne is tohopan he sceal habban on God; þonne he hwæt godes deð, þonne sceal he þæt he all tellan to Gode, and þonne he hwæt yfeles deð, he sceal witan þæt þæt cymð of him sylfum." [],"[001200 (4.126.28)] Domesdæg he geþencan sceal symle, and ellewitu he him sceal a ondrædan, and þæs ecan lifes he sceal mid ealre geornfulnysse gyrnan, and ælce dæge he him deaðes wenan." [],"[001300 (4.126.31)] His weorca he sceal giman on ælce tide, þæt þa gode syn, and he sceal geþencan, þæt he nahwær Gode digle ne bið, ac he hine æghwær gesihð; þa yfelan geþohtas, þe him on mod becumað, he sceal sona on Crist toslean and his gaslican lareowe andettan." [],"[001400 (4.126.35)] Þonne he hi toslehð on Criste, þonne he geþencað Cristes þrowunga and his wundra and mid þam geþohtum aflemð þa yfelan geþohtas." [],"[001500 (4.126.37)] His muð he sceal fram ælcum þweoran and yfelum wordum gehealdan; ne sceal he feala sprecan, ne yfel word ne leahtorbære; ne leahtor ne sceal he lufian." [],[001600 (4.127.39)] Halige rædinge he sceal lustlice gehyran and gelome on gebedum beon; his forðgewitenan yflu he sceal dæghwamlice mid tearum Gode andettan and hi georne betan. [],"[001700 (4.127.42)] Ne sceal he his lichaman luste folgian; his agene willan he sceal onscunian, and his abbodes bebodum on eallum þingum gehyrsumian, þeah hit si, swa swa hit ne gedafenað, þæt he wyrs do, þonne he lære; hi sceolon gemunon þone drihtenlican cwyde, on þam he cwæð: þæt þæt hie eow secgean, do ge þæt and þæt hi don, ne do ge hwæt." [],"[001800 (4.127.47)] Ne se man lofgeorn, ne ne wilnigende, þæt hys dæda halige gesæde syn, ær hie halige geweorðan ac hicge, þæt hit mid soðe gesæd beon mæge; ne sceal Godes bebodu dægwamlice mid godum dædum gyfelled and his clænnysse lufian; ne sceal he nanne man hatian; ne sceal he andian and æfst healdan, ne geflyt lufian; ne sceal uphafednesse forfleon and his ealdras wyrðian and his gingrun lufian; for Godes lufan for hyra fynd hie sceal gebiddan; ær sunnan setlgange hie scylon to sibbe fon wið þa ungeþweran; and he nære ne ortrywe be Godes miltse." [],"[001900 (4.128.56)] Efne nu þis synt gastlices cræftes tol and gebetla; þonne heo fram hus dæges and nihtes unablendelice beoð ealle gefyllede and on domesdæge eft betæhte, seo med us bið fram drihtne agolden, þe he sylfa behet, þæt his: þæt mænnissce eage for heora micelnysse geseon ne mihte; ne manne eare gehyran, ne on mannes heortan ne astah, þe God ælmihtig gegearwað eallum þam, þe hine lufiað." [],[002000 (4.128.62)] On mynstres claustre geferrædene staðolfæstnys ealle þas þenunge man began sceal and geornfullice wyrcean.
