[],"[008800 (44.397.1)] To myngianne is folc þæt hy to ðam þurhhaligum haligdome Drihtnes lichaman & blodes nateþeshwon unforwandienlice genealæcen, ne fram þam swiþe forhebben, ac anra gehwylc mid ealre geornfulnesse & gleawnesse geceose ða tid, hwænne he sume hwile fram gesynscyplicum weorce forhæbbe & fram leahtrum hine afeormige & mid mægenum gefrætwige & ælmessum & gebedum onwunige, & swa siððan mid myclum ege & arwyrþnesse to swa mærum haligdome genealæce." [],"[009000 (44.399.13)] Soþlice fram eallum gewisre tide is to gemænsumianne, buton ðam swiðe æfæstum & haliglice libbendum þe forneah ælce dæge þæt doð." [],"[009100 (45.401.1)] Ðæt þa mæssan ðe gind Sunnandagas sindrie fram mæssepreostum beoð, na swa in opennesse beon, þæt ðurh ða folc from openum mæssena symblum, þe on ðære þryddan tide regollice beoþ, beo fram abroden, for þam ðe wyrst gewuna is, oþþe on Sunnandagum oþþe on gehwylcum freolsdagum, sona swa hwylc gehyrð mæssan breman, eac swylce þeah heo beo for forðfarenum, he aweg gewit, & gynd ealne dæg fram ærnemergenne druncennisse & oferfyllum swyþor þonne Gode þeowaþ." [],"[009200 (46.403.10)] To myngianne is folc þæt hit ær openlicre & geendudre þenunge to mete ne genealæce, & þæt ealle to openlicre haligre meder, þæt is to þære halgan cyrcan, mæssena symblu & bodunge to gehyranne becumen, & mæssepreostas gynd gebedhus nateþeshwon buton swa wærlice ær þære oðre tide bremen, þæt þæt folc fram openlicum symblum ne sy framabroden." [],"[009300 (46.403.16)] Ac oþþe ða mæssepreostas ðe on þære burge embehwyrfte oððe on þære sylfan burg syndon, oþþe folc, swa we foresædon, on an to openlicre mæssena weorþunge tosomne becumen, buton Gode gehalgedum wifum, þam nis þæslic þæt hy to opennesse ut gan, ac hy sceolon on mynstres clusum beon behæfde."
